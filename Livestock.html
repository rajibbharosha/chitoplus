Chito Plus+ solution usage plan for Livestock

– Use after child prawn (one month), rate 20-40 cc. per water 100 cc. mix with animal feed 1 kg and lay down 1 hour before eat. 

-Poultry,Bird

 – Use at begin, rate 30-40 cc. per water 100 cc. mix with animal feed 1 kg, Lay down 1 hour before eat or mix only water rate 40 cc. per water 20 lites before eat. You can use both method ( with animal feed and only water ) 

– Cattle 

– Use at begin, rate 40 cc. per water 100 cc. mix with animal feed 1 kg, Lay down 1 hour before eat. or mix only rate 40 cc. per water 20 lites before eat.You can use both method ( with animal feed and only water )

Indication : can use mix every kind antibiotic. (Keep without sunshine and shake gallon before use)
